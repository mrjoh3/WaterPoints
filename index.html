Water Point Finder

Welcome to the local Water Point (WP) finder. This website uses your device location to identify an assist navigation to the nearest WP. For this to work you need to allow location access. Once your location is found all WP's within 5km will be shown on the map. If your location is unavailable all Nillumbik WP's will be shown.

  • Click on markers to view detailed information
  • Click on the "Directions" button (or double-click a marker) to get detailed driving directions.
  • Use the layer toggle to filter Water Points by type.
  • Drag the map or zoom to explore different areas.
  • Click the “?” button (top left) any time for help.
